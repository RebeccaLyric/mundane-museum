Welcome to the Museum of the Mundane!

This site provides an example of a simple webpage for a history museum or public history organization.

Here you will find a gallery of objects used in everyday 21st-century life, a page of resources for educators, and a contact page. The creator of this site has a background in history and museums. This is her first website, so she appreciates your feedback.

Click on the links at the top of the page or a picture above to get started!
